About

Where Science Meets
the Public

Dr. Tajudeen Yahaya is a Nigerian-based scientist and science communicator whose career sits at the intersection of rigorous academic research and accessible public education. As an Associate Professor in Biological Sciences, he brings decades of expertise in genetics, toxicology, pharmacology, phytomedicine, and environmental health.

"Translating complex scientific concepts into knowledge the world can act on."

Through his personal brand Tyo Biopedia, he operates as a digital content creator who distills cutting-edge research into content that students, health-conscious individuals, and the general public can understand and use.

With over 70 peer-reviewed publications, his research contributions are matched only by his commitment to ensuring science never stays locked behind academic walls.
